Dialog 6: — Kto stoi obok ________ (drzwi)? — To nasz sąsiad.
Vocabulary & Synonym Note (EN):
• obok = next to / beside (e.g. obok drzwi)
• blisko = close to / near (e.g. blisko drzwi)
• przy = at / by / right next to (e.g. przy drzwiach)
All three express proximity! Notice that obok and blisko take the Genitive case (drzwi), while przy takes the Locative case (drzwiach).
